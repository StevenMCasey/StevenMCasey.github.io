About me

Steven Michael Casey i was born in 1999 at Queen Mary's Hospital and from an early age i was diagnosed with cancer, during my time in hospital my love for video games grew.

I am currently studying at Bournemouth University on a four year game design course, previously i completed a two year degree in college studying 3D Modelling as well as 2D concept art.

Working part time as a freelance artists while in education, it has allowed me to gain more experience working with various teams of highly skilled artists on many projects, if you would like to see more of my assets please click the Projects tab above.

On the right hand side you can see a animated trialer I created for a project called fireflies.
